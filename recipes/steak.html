Steps to prepare a delicious steak

  1. Season the steak with salt up to 2 hrs before, then with pepper just before cooking.
  2. Heat the pan until it becomes very hot but do not allow it to smoke
  3. Drizzle some oil into the pan and leave for a moment.
  4. Add the steak, a knob of butter, some garlic and robust herbs, if you want.
  5. Sear evenly on each side for our recommended time, turning every minute for the best caramelised crust. Leave to rest on a board or warm plate for about 5 mins.
  6. Serve the steak whole or carved into slices with the resting juices poured over.
